Business Problem:
     The Business problem framed for this task to identify a best location of setting up a "Indian Restaurant" in Tornoto by an entrepreneur. 
     This problem could be solved by segmentation and clustering of suitable neighbourhood locations in Tornoto and performing K means clustering to find out the best spots.
     
Target Audience:
People who wish to start an Indian Restaurant at Tornato.
     
     
Data Required for Analysis:
1. Neighbourhood dataset of Tornoto city   (Could be obtained by Scraping the Wikepdia using BeautifulSoup)
2. Latitude and Longitude values of neighbourhoods  (Using Geocode Package)
3. Data associated with Indian Restaurant (Making Foursquare calls)
